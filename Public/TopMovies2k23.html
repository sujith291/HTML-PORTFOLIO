Best Movies of 2K23 so far...

My Top movies that I have watched this year

Waltair Veerayya

Starring South Indian actor Chiranjeevi, this movie has all the plots to make into the 1000 Cr club. The story has all the twists in it to be a box office blockbuster

Indiana Jones, The last crusade

This movie was the latest in the prestigious Indian Jones series which somehow seems to be the last time we seeing Indy in action. Better not to miss this roller coaster ride of a movie.

Top Gun: Maverick

Starring Tom Cruise this movie has all the sequences to make go wow in the theatres. Cool stunts with the jet planes and concluding some unfinished business.
